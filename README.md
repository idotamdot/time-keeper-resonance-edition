# 🌟 Time Keeper: Resonance Edition

A beautifully designed personal organization app to help you reclaim your **time**, **energy**, and **joy** — one star at a time.

---

## ✨ Features

- **Today View**: Set your energy level, focus your intention, and see your daily progress reflected in a glowing star meter.

- **Task Tracking**: Add, complete, and celebrate tasks with animated interactions and visual rewards.

- **Persistent Storage**: Your tasks and intentions are saved locally, so you can pick up right where you left off.

- **Star Meter**: Earn stars for each completed task — your day becomes a constellation of accomplishments.

- **Visual Joy**: Whimsical gradients, emojis, and glowing UI elements enhance your sense of flow and delight.

---

## 📦 Tech Stack

- **Frontend**: React + TypeScript + Vite
- **Styling**: CSS-in-JSX with custom gradients and animation
- **Backend**: Express (for optional task API or future syncing)
- **State**: useState, useEffect, and localStorage
